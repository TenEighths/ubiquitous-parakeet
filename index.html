Home

After feeling lost about my future, an encounter with a stranger inspired me to become a web developer. With a passion for critical thinking and problem solving, I fell in love with the field and aspire to continue growing and learning alongside the ever changing needs of the industry.

Having spent my life surrounded by technology I naturally developed a love of video games and movies, and I carried that passion into my adulthood. I channel my creativity through cooking and preparing meals for the people I love.

Education

I started my web development journey at Camosun College in Victoria BC, where I learned the fundamentals of web design and computer science. After graduating from the Computer Systems Technology diploma program, I furthered my education with Juno College's Web Development Bootcamp. At Juno I learned the nuances of design and front-end development, with a focus on inclusivity and accessibility.
